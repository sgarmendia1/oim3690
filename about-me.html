Artists

  • Jack Penny

    Jack Penny is a British contemporary artist known for his abstract paintings and mixed media works. His works typically depictcontemporary urban life from the perspective of a rural-dwelling outsider.

  • Werner Bronkhorst

    Werner Bronkhorst is a South African artist whose work explores themes of of human interacting in nature. His paintings often feature vibrant colors and intricate patterns as well as textured paint on a canvas to depict nature.

  • Jack Kabangu

    Jack Kabangu is a Zambian artist who creates paintings that blend his African heritage with contemporary elements. His works feature vibrant colors, dynamic lines, and a mix of figurative and abstract styles, drawing comparisons with the spontaneity of Abstract Expressionism and graffiti art.
